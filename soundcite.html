Examples of sounds without and with hearing loss.

These examples of different hearing tries to simulate how hearing loss can turn out.

  • Normal hearing is a reference of how young people and and adults music without hearing loss will perceive the music.
  • Moderate hearing loss recreate how a person with moderate hearing loss will perceive the music.
  • Severe hearing loss recreate how a person with severe hearing loss will perceive the music.
The first example is music without hearing loss
