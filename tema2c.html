Hearing impairment

About 20% of people with hearing loss would benefit from devices such as hearing aids and cochlear implants.

How to use a hearing aid

The hearing aid is a sophisticated audio amplifier which to some extent can compensate for hearing loss. Since hearing loss is individual, hearing aid must be adapted to each user. The hearings aid amplifiers not all sounds like a lot, but emphasizes the sounds that the user has difficulty perceiving. But the soundscape emanating from the hearing aid is not as clearly as the soundscape a person with normal hearing experience. Therefore hearing aid users should get used to the sound image from the instrument and learn how to interpret this.
